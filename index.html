Privacy Policy – Faith Quest

Last updated: April 2, 2026

Faith Quest ("we", "our", or "us") is a free mobile game available on Android. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected and used when you play Faith Quest.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or location.

Faith Quest uses Google AdMob to display banner and rewarded advertisements. AdMob may collect certain device information automatically, including:

  • Advertising ID (used to serve relevant ads)
  • Device type and operating system version
  • IP address (used to determine approximate location for ad targeting)
  • Ad interaction data (e.g. whether an ad was viewed or clicked)

This data is collected and processed by Google LLC. We do not have direct access to this information.

How We Use Information

Game progress (completed levels, coins, settings) is stored locally on your device only using Android's SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party.

Advertising data collected by AdMob is used solely to display advertisements within the app.

Third-Party Services

Faith Quest uses Google AdMob for advertising. Google's use of advertising data is governed by the Google Privacy Policy.

You can opt out of personalised advertising by adjusting your device settings under Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation.

Children's Privacy

Faith Quest is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app displays ads through AdMob configured for a general audience.

Data Retention

All game data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all locally stored game data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
